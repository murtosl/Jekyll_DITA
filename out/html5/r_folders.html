Jekyll Folders

Jekyll expects certain types of files to be stored in specific folders. There are six folders that need to be created.

Folders

Name Stores
_data Data files (like CSV, YML or JSON)
_drafts Unpublished posts
_includes Snippets of HTML or posts that can be reused
_layouts The various layouts for the website
_posts Completed posts
_site Automatically used when Jekyll makes your completed website
